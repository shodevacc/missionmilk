About

What is Mission Milk?

Mission Milk is simply put a milk bank. Our goal us to provide children with the most basic source of nutrition that we have all grown up with as indian kids, MILK.

Why Milk?

It all started with an effort to deliver the basic rations to as many families as we could. During one such visit we realized at a single settlement about 50 families, there is an average of 4 children per family aged between 10 months to 10 years. WHile discussing their needs we understood that although most of the adults needs were being met through the rations being provided, the children still needed milk.

How Does Mission Milk Work?

if you ever wondered how you do you part in helping out the people on need during this period of lock down in the safest and most efficient way possible, this is it. We need you to enable us to help these children on your behalf. We are looking to collect donations corresponding to the value of per liter of milk. It is imperitive that you, our donors, understand that this is a daily requirement. Our supply will vary day to day as per the requests that we receive and funds raised. We intend on providing fresh milk, not only to the families we are already helping but any and all organisations. NGO's and other such amazing people already providing support to the needy.

Work

We started our journey making our very first delivery of fresh milk.

We started with about 60 liters of milk and were able to provide milk for 70 Families

Though it seemed impossible at the time. Thanks to our milk angels, who donated and helped us to stay active, we have been able to increase our inventory to about 500 liters of milk a day!

Effectively avergaed to feed about 1300 children a day by Day 47.

We are still continuing our journey. We hope you'll decide to be a part of this change as well!

If you do then pitch in! Lets make a brighter future together!
